# Capstone Project — The Killer Application Asset

**This is the single most important deliverable of the 6 months.** More than grades (you have none), more than papers read, more than any single course. The capstone is what makes your application memorable.

---

## Why the Capstone Matters

1. **Demonstrates research capability** — design, execute, analyze, write
2. **Shows initiative** — most conversion applicants don't have a project
3. **Proves your "why"** — your topic reveals what you actually care about
4. **Conversation starter** — interview fodder, supervisor match signal
5. **Optional publication** — strong capstones can be submitted to student journals

---

## Topic Selection Criteria (SMART)

- **S**pecific: One clear research question, not "I want to study psychology"
- **M**easurable: Variables you can operationalize + measure with a survey
- **A**chievable: N feasible for solo research (50–200), methods doable in 2 weeks
- **R**elevant: Connects to your background + subfield + HKU faculty
- **T**ime-bound: Completes in 4 weeks of data collection + 2 weeks of write-up

## Project Ideas (pick or adapt)

### 1. "Personality and Decision-Making Style in Engineering Professionals" (JDM + Personality)
- **Sample:** 100+ engineers (your network, LinkedIn, r/civilengineering)
- **Measures:** Big Five (IPIP-NEO-120) + General Decision-Making Style (GDMS) + Domain-Specific Risk-Taking (DOSPERT)
- **Research questions:**
  - RQ1: Which Big Five traits predict analytical vs intuitive decision styles?
  - RQ2: Does risk-taking in engineering correlate with risk-taking in financial contexts?
- **Why strong:** Quant-friendly, personality, JDM, your network, fast recruitment

### 2. "Self-Regulation and Adherence in Solo Athletic Training" (Health Psych)
- **Sample:** 80+ recreational athletes (MMA, gym, running communities)
- **Measures:** Self-Regulation Questionnaire (SRQ) + Brief Self-Control Scale + Sport Motivation Scale + adherence log
- **Research questions:**
  - RQ1: Does self-regulation predict training adherence over 4 weeks?
  - RQ2: What psychological factors differentiate high-adherers from low-adherers?
- **Why strong:** MMA angle, behavioral, longitudinal, applied

### 3. "Metacognition in Self-Directed Multi-Domain Learning" (Educational Psych)
- **Sample:** 80+ polymaths / multi-domain self-learners
- **Measures:** Metacognitive Awareness Inventory (MAI) + Self-Regulated Learning (SRL) + learning log
- **Research questions:**
  - RQ1: How do successful multi-domain self-learners monitor their own learning?
  - RQ2: What metacognitive strategies correlate with breadth and depth of learning?
- **Why strong:** Personal (you are the case), educational psych, novel angle

### 4. "Stress, Personality, and Risk Preferences in Solo Trading" (JDM + Behavioral Finance)
- **Sample:** 60+ retail traders (with ethical review considerations)
- **Measures:** Perceived Stress Scale (PSS) + Big Five + DOSPERT + behavioral trading log
- **Research questions:**
  - RQ1: Does stress predict risk-taking in simulated trading tasks?
  - RQ2: Do personality traits moderate the stress-risk relationship?
- **Why strong:** Trading angle, behavioral finance, quantitative
- **Risk:** Mental health considerations in research design

### 5. "Trust and Acceptance of AI Decision Aids" (Cognitive + Human-AI Interaction)
- **Sample:** 100+ professionals across industries
- **Measures:** Trust in Automation (TiA) + Propensity to Trust + domain + AI use frequency
- **Research questions:**
  - RQ1: How does domain expertise moderate trust in AI recommendations?
  - RQ2: What personality factors predict AI acceptance?
- **Why strong:** AI Agents interest, novel, fast data collection
